Overview
This page offers a collection of visualizations for exploring the ribosomal complex 3CC2 (large subunit, 23S chain, H. marismortui). 3CC2 is initially loaded at right; use the buttons below to change the type of visualization as well as other visualization options. (Please be patient as the files take a little bit of time to load.) The yellow point on the screen (when visible) indicates the geometric center of the chain. Hover over the visualization and visualization option buttons to see a description of the functions they perform.
